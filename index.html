Explore Kanpur

Kanpur

The City of Nawabs & Leather

While you're here for the festivities, take a moment to explore the vibrant heritage and hidden gems of Kanpur.

Allen Forest Zoo

One of the few zoos in India established within a natural forest, featuring a large lake and diverse wildlife including lions, tigers, and exotic birds.

⭐ 4.2 · Open Tue–Sun, 9am–5pm

JK Temple

A stunning white marble temple dedicated to Lord Radha Krishna, celebrated for its harmonious blend of Nagara-style ancient architecture with modern design sensibility.

⭐ 4.6 · Open 5–12am & 4–10pm daily

Moti Jheel

A scenic lake and parkland originally built as a drinking water reservoir — now beloved for peaceful boating, evening strolls, and lush green surroundings.

⭐ 4.2 · Open daily, ₹10 entry

Kanpur Memorial Church

An impressive Lombardic Gothic cathedral built in 1875, commemorating those who fell during the 1857 uprising. Serene, majestic, and beautifully preserved.

⭐ 4.4 · Visit Sun 9–11am · Carry ID

Bithoor

A mythological town on the sacred banks of the Ganges — believed to be the birthplace of sage Valmiki and a key site in Indian mythology. A deeply spiritual half-day trip.

📍 ~20km from Kanpur city centre
Getting Here

Getting to Kanpur

Travel guide for guests

We want your journey to be as comfortable as the celebration itself. Here's everything you need to know.

✈️

By Air

Fly into Kanpur Airport (KNU) or Lucknow Chaudhary Charan Singh International Airport (LKO), which is approximately 90 minutes away. Pre-paid taxis and app cabs are readily available from both airports.

🚂

By Train

Kanpur Central Station (CNB) is one of the busiest rail junctions in India, with excellent connectivity from Delhi (Shatabdi/Rajdhani), Mumbai, and Kolkata. The hotel is approximately 20 minutes by taxi from the station.

🚗

By Road

Kanpur is well-connected via the Agra–Lucknow Expressway. Approximately 4.5 hours from Delhi, 1.5 hours from Lucknow, and 2 hours from Agra. Ample parking is available at Landmark Towers.

🏨

Venue & Accommodation

All events are hosted at Landmark Towers, 10 The Mall, Civil Lines, Kanpur. Rooms are available at the hotel — contact us to receive the group booking details and special rates.
